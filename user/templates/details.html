How Confidential Data Is Handled

  • The Application only stores your aadhaar number, name and dob in the database after you cast your vote. These details are stored to allow only one vote per aadhaar number.

  • The Application does not store your photograph.

  • The Application does not store your uploaded aadhaar card image.

  • Your uploaded aadhaar card image is only used to scan the QR code for aadhaar verification and face matching for face verification.

Aadhaar Image Upload Guidelines

  • After successful Aadhaar Verification, webcam will be used for face verfication. Ensure that your face is clearly visible.

  • Upload A Clear Image Of Your Aadhaar Card.

  • QR Code must be properly visible.

  • Aadhaar Card image with Normal QR code must be uploaded. (NOT SECURE QR)
